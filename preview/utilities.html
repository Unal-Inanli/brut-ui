Responsive variants

Append -sm-, -md-, or -lg- for mobile-first responsive behavior (min-width breakpoints).

Examples:

  • d-none d-md-flex — hidden on mobile, flex on md+
  • flex-column flex-md-row — stacks on mobile, row on md+
  • p-3 p-md-5 p-lg-7 — padding increases at each breakpoint
  • mt-4 mt-md-0 — top margin on mobile, none on md+
  • text-center text-md-start — centered on mobile, left on md+
  • w-100 w-md-50 w-lg-25 — width narrows as viewport grows

Breakpoints

sm ≥ 640px
md ≥ 768px
lg ≥ 1024px
